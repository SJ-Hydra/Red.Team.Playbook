## Questions

- Can you please describe your network at a high level?

- How many hosts do you have?
  - What kind of OSs are used in the environment?

- What type of services are you offering internally? (FTP server, etc.)

- What kind of browsers are in use by the users?

- What type of security products are you using on endpoints, mail delivery, and on the network?

- Does the network traffic particularly sensitive data (e.g. PCI)?

- Are there are assets that are considered out of scope or should be avoided?

- For Red Teaming, what ingress methods will you permit? (e.g. phishing, physical access[thumb drives], vishing, etc.)
